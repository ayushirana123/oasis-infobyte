Sushant Singh Rajput

1986-2020

SSR

Sushant Singh Rajput was an Indian actor who gained fame through his work in Bollywood films. He was born on January 21, 1986, and sadly passed away on June 14, 2020. Sushant started his acting career on television and later made a successful transition to the big screen. He was known for his charming personality, good looks, and versatile acting skills. Sushant starred in several notable films such as "Kai Po Che!", "M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story," and "Chhichhore." He was praised for his performances and garnered a strong fan following. Apart from acting, Sushant was also passionate about science, technology, and astronomy. He was known to be an intellectual and curious person, constantly seeking knowledge and exploring various interests. Tragically, Sushant's life ended too soon, and his untimely death shocked his fans and the entertainment industry. He is remembered as a talented actor whose work continues to be appreciated by many.

Biographies

  • Early Life: Sushant was born into a Rajput family and grew up in Patna. He had four elder sisters. He was academically bright and had an early interest in science and technology.
  • Education: Sushant attended St. Karen's High School in Patna and later moved to Delhi for further studies. He enrolled at the Delhi College of Engineering (now Delhi Technological University) to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering.
  • Turning Point: During his college days, Sushant discovered his passion for acting and dance. He was actively involved in theater and dance groups and performed in various college events and competitions. However, he dropped out of college in the third year to pursue a career in acting.
  • Television Career: Sushant's journey in the entertainment industry began in 2008 when he was selected for the dance-based reality show "Zara Nachke Dikha." He gained recognition for his dancing skills and was subsequently cast in the popular TV serial "Kis Desh Mein Hai Meraa Dil." However, his breakthrough came with the role of Manav in the soap opera "Pavitra Rishta," which aired from 2009 to 2011. His portrayal of Manav earned him widespread fame and a massive fan following.
  • Bollywood Debut: Sushant's talent and popularity on television caught the attention of filmmakers, leading to his Bollywood debut in 2013. He played one of the lead roles in the film "Kai Po Che!," which was based on Chetan Bhagat's novel "The 3 Mistakes of My Life." The film was a critical and commercial success, and Sushant's performance was highly praised by critics and audiences.
  • Interests and Passions: Sushant had a curious and inquisitive mind. Apart from acting, he had a keen interest in science, technology, and astronomy. He was fascinated by space exploration and owned a telescope to observe celestial bodies. He was also an avid reader and loved to explore various subjects.
  • Achievements: Throughout his career, Sushant received several awards for his acting performances, including Best Male Debut and Best Actor awards at various award ceremonies.
  • Personal Traits: Sushant was often described as a charming, humble, and down-to-earth person by his colleagues and friends. He was known for his warm smile and approachable nature. He had a positive outlook on life and believed in following his dreams passionately.
  • Legacy: Sushant's fans and peers remember him fondly for his exceptional talent, intellectual pursuits, and contributions to the world of cinema. He continues to be celebrated as a talented actor whose work left a lasting impact on audiences.
